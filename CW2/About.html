The Luxury Art Fair is the art marketplace

We sell art that changes lives. Art that awakens our imagination, transforms our homes and expresses who we really are. At The Luxury Art Fair we’re building a world where artists can make a living doing what they love. Where anyone can easily find and buy original art, no matter their taste or budget.

Direct from the artist to your home

Whether you’re an art lover or art curious, we connect you directly with independent artists around the world. Every single piece is original and signed by the artist, carrying its own unique story.

A sustainable art market for all

We founded The Luxury Art Fair because artists exist everywhere - as do people who value authentic, handmade works of art. We believe in ‘made to last,’ in ethical production, in building a business that makes all of our lives better by supporting creativity in communities around the world.
